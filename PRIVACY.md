# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 2025

This document provides a summary of our privacy practices. For the full, legally binding Privacy Policy, see:
**[https://fabrk.dev/privacy](https://fabrk.dev/privacy)**

## Summary

### What We Collect
- **Account Information**: Email, name (for authentication)
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely via Stripe/Polar (we don't store card details)
- **Usage Data**: Analytics for product improvement (via PostHog)
- **Technical Data**: Browser type, IP address (for security/debugging)

### How We Use It
- Provide and improve our services
- Process payments and deliver purchases
- Send important account notifications
- Analyze aggregate usage patterns

### Your Rights
- Access and export your data
- Request data deletion
- Opt out of marketing communications
- Update your account information

### Third-Party Services
We use the following trusted services:
- **Stripe/Polar**: Payment processing
- **PostHog**: Product analytics
- **Resend**: Transactional emails
- **Vercel**: Hosting infrastructure

### Security
- All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
- Database encryption at rest
- Regular security audits
- SOC 2 compliant hosting

### Contact
For privacy questions: **privacy@fabrk.dev**
