I'm excited to finally launch **Fabrk** - a Next.js SaaS boilerplate I've been building for the past 6 months.

I've been building SaaS products for 3 years. Every time I started a new project, I faced the same choice:

1. **Build from scratch** - 3-4 weeks of auth, payments, database setup before building actual features
2. **Use ShipFast ($199)** - Great product, but JavaScript-only (no TypeScript), MongoDB-only
3. **Use Supastarter ($349)** - Feature-complete but 800+ files, overwhelming for MVPs

None felt right. I wanted:
- ✅ **TypeScript strict mode** (type safety from day one)
- ✅ **PostgreSQL** (more popular for SaaS than MongoDB)
- ✅ **Latest stack** (Next.js 15, NextAuth v5)
- ✅ **Essential-only** (no 1000-file maze)
- ✅ **Fair pricing** (not $199-349)

So I built Fabrk.
