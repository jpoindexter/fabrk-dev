# Writing Tips

## Best Practices

1. **Lead with numbers**: Start with impressive metrics
2. **Use quotes**: Real customer voice is more credible than marketing copy
3. **Be specific**: "3 weeks faster" > "much faster"
4. **Show the before/after**: Contrast makes impact clear
5. **Explain the why**: Why did Fabrk specifically help?

## Case Study Objectives

- ✅ Demonstrate real ROI
- ✅ Show time-to-value
- ✅ Highlight cost savings
- ✅ Build social proof
- ✅ Inspire other customers
