# Case Study Template

> **Want to be featured here?** Tell us your Fabrk success story!

---

## Your Success Story Goes Here

We're looking for Fabrk customers who want to share their experience building with our boilerplate.

**What we're looking for:**
- How you used Fabrk to ship faster
- Time/cost savings compared to building from scratch
- Features you found most valuable
- Your journey from idea to launch

**Benefits of being featured:**
- Backlink to your product/website
- Exposure to our developer community
- Highlight your technical achievements

---

## How to Submit

Email us at **hello@fabrek.dev** with:

1. **Your product**: Name, URL, one-liner
2. **Timeline**: How long did it take to build?
3. **Key metrics**: Time saved, cost savings, launch date
4. **Quote**: A testimonial we can feature
5. **Screenshot**: Your product in action

---

## Case Study Format

Once approved, we'll work with you to create a case study following this structure:

### Executive Summary
- Company/Product name
- Industry
- Challenge faced
- How Fabrk helped
- Key result

### The Challenge
- What problem you were solving
- Why you chose Fabrk

### The Solution
- Which Fabrk features you used
- Your implementation timeline

### The Results
- Quantifiable metrics (time, cost, speed)
- Quote from your team

### About Your Product
- Brief description
- Website link
- Contact info (optional)
