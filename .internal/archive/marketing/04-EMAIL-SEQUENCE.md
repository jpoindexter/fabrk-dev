## Email Setup Notes

### Timing
- Email 1: Launch day (morning)
- Email 2: 2-3 days after launch
- Email 3: 4-5 days after launch
- Email 4: 7 days after launch

### Segmentation
- Only send to people who haven't purchased
- Remove purchasers from sequence immediately
- Consider re-engagement for non-openers

### Links
Replace `[GET FABRK NOW →]` with your actual product URL with UTM parameters:
```
https://yoursite.com?utm_source=email&utm_medium=launch&utm_campaign=email1
```

### Testing
- Send test emails to yourself first
- Check all links work
- Preview on mobile
- Test subject lines with small segment
